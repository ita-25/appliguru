How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: December 24, 2024

At Appliguru, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our job application services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register for our services, we collect:

  • Full name and contact information (email, phone number, WhatsApp)
  • Resume/CV and professional background
  • Educational qualifications and certifications
  • Work experience and employment history
  • Skills, competencies, and professional achievements
  • Job preferences (desired titles, industries, locations, salary expectations)
  • Career goals and deal breakers

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring website addresses
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

1.3 Application Tracking Information

We maintain records of:

  • Jobs we've applied to on your behalf
  • Application dates and submission details
  • Interview invitations and outcomes
  • Communication with employers regarding your applications

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Delivery

  • To provide our job application concierge services
  • To search for and match job opportunities to your profile
  • To tailor and submit job applications on your behalf
  • To optimize your resume and LinkedIn profile
  • To communicate with potential employers on your behalf
  • To track and report on application progress

2.2 Service Improvement

  • To improve our job matching algorithms
  • To enhance our application submission processes
  • To analyze service effectiveness and success rates
  • To personalize your experience with our services

2.3 Communication

  • To send you application updates and progress reports
  • To notify you of interview opportunities
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To send service-related announcements
  • To request feedback on our services (with your consent)

2.4 Legal and Security

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • To enforce our Terms of Service
  • To resolve disputes and troubleshoot problems

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Prospective Employers

We share your resume, cover letter, and relevant professional information with employers only when submitting job applications on your behalf. This is the core purpose of our service and is authorized by you when you register.

3.2 Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business, such as:

  • Cloud storage and hosting services
  • Email communication platforms
  • Payment processors
  • Analytics services

These providers are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the services they provide to us.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others
  • Investigate fraud or security issues

3.4 We Do NOT Sell Your Information

Appliguru never sells, rents, or trades your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Employee confidentiality agreements
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Update your job preferences and profile at any time

5.2 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal information by contacting us. Please note that:

  • We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes
  • Deletion of your account will terminate our services
  • Some information may remain in backup systems for a limited time

5.3 Opt-Out of Communications

You can opt out of non-essential communications by:

  • Clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails
  • Contacting us directly at appliguru@gmail.com

Note that you cannot opt out of essential service-related communications (e.g., application updates, account notifications).

5.4 Service Termination

You may terminate our services at any time by contacting us. Upon termination, we will:

  • Stop submitting applications on your behalf
  • Retain your data for a limited period as required by law
  • Delete your information upon request (subject to legal obligations)

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Provide personalized content

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as:

  • You maintain an active account with us
  • Necessary to provide our services
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

After you terminate your account, we may retain certain information in accordance with legal requirements and for legitimate business purposes such as dispute resolution and fraud prevention.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (for material changes)

Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

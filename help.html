FAQ

Getting Started

How do I get started with Appliguru?
Getting started is simple! Just fill out our registration form on the Contact page with your job preferences and career information. After submission, our team will review your profile and contact you within 24 hours to confirm your plan and begin the application process.
What information do I need to provide during registration?
You'll need to provide:
  • Personal contact information
  • Desired job titles and industries
  • Experience level and education
  • Preferred job locations
  • Employment type preferences (full-time, remote, etc.)
  • Salary expectations
  • Key skills and certifications
  • Your current resume/CV (sent via email)
How long does it take to start receiving applications?
Once your profile is confirmed and we receive your resume, we typically begin submitting applications within 3-5 business days. You'll receive your first batch of applications according to your selected plan schedule.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Application Process

How many applications will you submit per month?
This depends on your plan:
  • Basic Plan: 3 applications per week (approximately 6 per month)
  • Standard Plan: 4 applications per week (approximately 12 per month)
  • Premium Plan: Unlimited applications based on suitable opportunities
How do you choose which jobs to apply for?
We use the detailed preferences you provide during registration. Our intelligent matching system filters jobs based on your desired titles, industries, location, salary range, experience level, and any deal breakers you've specified. We prioritize quality matches over quantity.
Will I know which jobs you've applied to?
Absolutely! We provide detailed tracking of all applications submitted on your behalf, including company name, job title, application date, and job description. Standard and Premium plans receive weekly progress reports.
How do you tailor my resume for each application?
Our team analyzes each job description and optimizes your resume with relevant keywords, skills, and achievements that match the position. We ensure your resume passes ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) while remaining authentic to your experience and qualifications.
Can I approve applications before they're submitted?
Our service is designed to save you time by handling the application process independently. However, if you have specific companies you want to avoid or special requirements, include them in your "deal breakers" during registration. Premium plan clients can request application reviews.

Interview Support

Do you help with interview preparation?
Yes! Standard and Premium plans include interview tips assistance. We provide company research, common interview questions for your role, STAR method guidance, and follow-up email templates. Premium plan includes a one-on-one interview coaching session.
What happens when I get an interview?
Once you receive an interview invitation, notify us immediately. We'll provide you with preparation materials specific to that company and role. Standard and Premium clients receive interview tips assistance to help you prepare effectively.

Privacy & Security

Is my information secure?
Yes! We take data security very seriously. Your personal information and resume are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties except for the specific job applications you've approved through our service. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
Will my current employer find out I'm job searching?
We maintain strict confidentiality. If you specify your current employer in your "deal breakers," we'll ensure no applications are submitted to that company. We also recommend keeping your job search discreet on social media.

Technical Questions

Which job boards do you search?
We search all major Nigerian and international job boards including LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Jobberman, Hot Nigerian Jobs, and company career pages. Our system monitors thousands of postings daily to find opportunities that match your profile.
Do you handle international job applications?
Yes! If you're open to international opportunities or remote positions with foreign companies, just include this in your location preferences during registration. We can apply to positions worldwide.
How do I update my job preferences?
Simply contact us via email or phone/WhatsApp anytime you need to update your preferences, deal breakers, or any other information. We'll update your profile immediately.
